Privacy Policy

At Conversable, we build deliberate, minimalist social learning tools. We are committed to protecting your personal data, simulated inputs, and privacy rights. This policy outlines what we collect, how it is secured, and your control over your data.

Information We Collect

We believe in absolute data minimization. We only collect the minimal personal data necessary to provide a high-fidelity simulator experience and help you track your progress.

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect your email address, name, and password (securely salted and hashed). This data is used solely to authenticate your identity, secure your dashboard, and prevent service abuse.

Conversation Data

As you run simulated negotiations or conversations in the playground, the choices you select are processed to build your behavioral feedback. This data is only linked to your account to map historical improvements.

AI Analysis Data

We analyze your simulation choices using secure, isolated large language models to construct communication reports. This analysis checks clarity, confidence, and empathy levels. We do not use your simulated conversation data to train public foundation models.

Uploaded Files

If you upload custom conversation scripts or scenario files, these files are encrypted at rest and accessible only by you and the specific services required to parse them for your simulator sessions.

Device Information

To optimize display layouts and debug system faults, we log device type, operating system version, browser user agent, language settings, and IP address. This data is cleaned of personal identifiers and deleted within 30 days.

Cookies

We use essential cookies to maintain your login session. We do not use third-party tracking, retargeting, or advertising cookies. You can manage cookies in your browser settings at any time.

Analytics

We collect anonymized product telemetry to understand how our features are used. This data contains no IP addresses or personal identifiers, keeping your activity fully private.

Payment Information

Payment information is processed securely by Stripe, our third-party billing provider. Conversable does not store, transmit, or have access to your credit card number or verification details.

Third-Party Services

We partner with secure cloud infrastructure and AI inference providers. Every provider is carefully vetted and bound by strict data processing agreements ensuring compliance with confidentiality laws.

How We Use Information

Your data is used to provide the simulator, generate communication analytics, process subscription renewals, resolve technical support tickets, and implement security controls to prevent bot attacks or spam.

Data Retention

We keep your personal information only as long as you maintain an active account. When you delete your account, your data is deleted within the timelines outlined in our Data Deletion Policy.

Security

We apply high-grade physical and digital security, including AES-256 encryption at rest, TLS 1.3 transit encryption, and continuous vulnerability scanning. Details are described in our Security Policy.

International Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your residence (primarily the EU and the United States), where cloud facilities are subject to rigorous privacy safeguards.

Children's Privacy

Our platform is directed to users who are at least 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under the age of 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with data, contact us.

User Rights

Depending on your location, you have rights to access, update, correct, restrict, download, or delete your personal data. We honor these rights globally, regardless of your local jurisdiction.

Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time through your dashboard profile settings, which immediately triggers the permanent deletion of your profile and history.

Contact Information

For questions or requests regarding your personal data, email our privacy team at privacy@conversable.app.

Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how Conversable uses cookies and similar technologies to recognize you when you visit our website. It details what these cookies are and how you can control them.

What Cookies Are

Cookies are small data files placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used by website owners to make their websites work, or work more efficiently, as well as to provide reporting information.

Essential Cookies

These cookies are strictly necessary to provide you with services available through our platform and to use some of its features, such as access to secure authenticated areas.

Analytics Cookies

These cookies collect information that is used in aggregate form to help us understand how our website is being used or how effective our platform performance is, helping us improve the user experience.

Preference Cookies

We use preference cookies to remember your settings (such as dark mode choice, simulator volume, or your selected coaching language), so you do not have to configure them on every visit.

Third-Party Cookies

In addition to our own cookies, secure third parties (specifically our payment processor Stripe) may set cookies to handle fraud prevention, verify transactions, and ensure secure payments.

Managing Cookies

You have the right to decide whether to accept or reject cookies. You can set or amend your web browser controls to accept or refuse cookies. If you choose to reject cookies, you may still use our website, though your access to some functionality and secure areas may be restricted.

Browser Settings

Most browsers allow you to block, delete, or manage cookies through their settings menu. Please refer to your browser's help documentation (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge) for specific instructions.

AI Usage Policy

This AI Usage Policy defines the scope, limitations, and responsibilities governing the use of artificial intelligence models within the Conversable simulator and reporting features.

AI-Generated Responses

All conversation partners and feedback reports are generated using large language models. The dialog options, responses, and score evaluations are calculated in real-time by algorithmic structures.

AI Limitations

Generative AI models are predictive systems trained on public data. They do not possess consciousness, human empathy, or objective understanding. They can occasionally exhibit biases or hallucinate incorrect details.

No Guaranteed Accuracy

We do not guarantee the objective correctness or validity of the simulator's responses or scoring feedback. Communication reports are directional recommendations rather than formal psychological metrics.

Human Responsibility

You retain full agency and responsibility for your real-world interactions. Do not make high-stakes business, professional, or personal decisions based solely on the scores or simulation outcomes generated by Conversable.

Educational Purpose

The platform is designed exclusively for self-directed practice, educational simulations, and soft-skills modeling. It is a sandbox to test styles, not a definitive predictor of social success.

Communication Coaching Disclaimer

Conversable feedback is intended for coaching purposes. It does not replace professional therapy, executive counseling, or specialized interpersonal relationship advice.

No Legal Advice

Although you can practice legal-themed negotiations or compliance conversations, Conversable's simulator and AI feedback do not constitute legal advice or formal representation.

No Medical Advice

The AI coaching metrics and interactions are not therapy, clinical psychology, medical advice, or psychiatric treatment. If you are experiencing social anxiety that impacts your health, consult a licensed healthcare professional.

No Financial Advice

Salary negotiation simulations are styled for practice. AI feedback regarding compensations, valuations, or contracts is not financial, investment, or career advisement.

Data Deletion Policy

At Conversable, we believe your data belongs to you. This policy outlines how you can delete your data, what is removed, what is retained, and the recovery timeline.

How Users Delete Their Account

You can delete your account instantly by navigating to your Account Settings page and clicking "Delete Account". Alternatively, you can request account deletion by emailing our support desk at support@conversable.app.

What Data Is Removed

Upon deletion, all personally identifiable information (PII) is removed. This includes your name, email, avatar, hashed password, completed conversation logs, performance metrics, and coaching history.

What Data Is Retained

We retain transaction histories and invoices for tax compliance as required by global financial regulations. Additionally, we retain anonymized, aggregated simulator usage statistics to maintain infrastructure logs. This statistical data cannot be linked back to you.

Deletion Timeline

The account deletion process begins immediately. All active databases purge your user profile within 7 business days. Backup storage files are systematically overwritten and cleared within 30 days.

Recovery Period

We maintain a 72-hour grace period during which you can cancel your deletion request by contacting support. After 72 hours, the database purge begins and the deletion is irreversible.

Authentication

Infrastructure

Conversable runs on premium cloud infrastructure providers that hold ISO 27001, SOC 2 Type II, and FedRAMP security certifications, ensuring round-the-clock physical protection of servers.

Monitoring

We implement automated intrusion detection systems and log auditing tools that monitor API endpoints for anomalous activity. Suspicious behaviors trigger immediate ops team alerts.

Accessibility Statement

Conversable is dedicated to building inclusive tools. We aim to ensure that our website, simulator, and legal resources are accessible to everyone, including individuals with disabilities.

Commitment to Accessibility

We strive to make Conversable conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA. We design our visual systems to ensure readability, clarity, and ease of use.

Keyboard Navigation

Our website, options menu, and simulator components are fully navigable using a keyboard. Users can navigate sections using Tab and activate controls with Enter or Space.

Screen Readers

We write semantic HTML structures with appropriate ARIA tags (including aria-live for dynamic simulator status changes) to support text-to-speech software (VoiceOver, NVDA, JAWS).

Contrast

We utilize high-contrast, minimalist palettes (pure white surfaces, dark gray borders, pure black text) to ensure a minimum contrast ratio of 4.5:1, fulfilling and exceeding WCAG standards.

Responsive Design

Our layouts scale fluidly. Users can zoom their browser window up to 300% without layout overlapping or losing control buttons.

Continuous Improvements

We test our site regularly using automated accessibility scanners and manual user flows. If you experience barriers, please contact us at accessibility@conversable.app so we can resolve them.
